A Little Background

Based in Lancaster, KY; Moon Eyed Imagineering is a specialty chemical and process development company. MEICO, is committed to doing whatever it takes to deliver results for our customers. To that end, we are engaged in a wide scope of projects and services, with one extremely focused purpose: to deliver innovative solutions to our customers, everyday.

Why can't we solve this problem? Why isn't this possible? Why not?

When our competition might shrug their shoulders or throw their hands up, we start asking why? Why can't we solve this problem? Why isn't this possible? Why not? It is an approach that has guided us to become the diverse company we are today. Specializing not only in innovative industrial and institutional chemical development, but also the design, acquisition, and implementation of equipment for the processes necessary for the use of our products.
